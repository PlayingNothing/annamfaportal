Drop your media assets here.

Usage examples:
- Hero background: `style={{ backgroundImage: \"url(/media/data-map.png)\" }}`
- Image tag: `<img src=\"/media/photo-id.png\" alt=\"Analyst\" />`
- Video iframe/embed: `<iframe src=\"https://www.youtube.com/embed/...\" />` (YouTube stays remote)
- Direct MP4: `<video src=\"/media/clip.mp4\" controls />`

Anything in /public is served at site root (`/media/...`).
